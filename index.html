Our Story

Building Trust
Since 2003.

Commercial Site Services was founded in the High Desert with one purpose: to give local businesses a dependable partner for keeping their properties clean, safe, and operational. No corporate runaround — just experienced crews who answer the phone and follow through.

Over two decades later, we've built long-term relationships with property managers, retail chains, developers, and municipalities across the Apple Valley region. Our reputation is earned job by job, not promised upfront.
